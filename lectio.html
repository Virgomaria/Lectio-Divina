LECTIO

Come into His presence and call upon the Spirit.
  • Lectio -- Holy Reading (mainly scripture, although you can use spiritual classics)
  • Think in terms of “grazing like a cow.” The cow is in no hurry.
  • Reading and Re-Reading silent or out-loud.
  • Use a short reading. No more than 10 -20 verses.
  • If you are gifted with several languages, read in both languages.
  • “Lectio is the eager seeking after the word of God and divine truth and the way by which God’s truth is imparted to us.”
