MEDITATIO

Listening for 15 -20 minutes; wait for the Lord.
  • Meditatio – Meditation and listening for God.
  • Think again of the cow. Think of how he chews his food. Once again in no hurry.
  • May include commentaries.
  • May include research.
  • Bringing the word of God and transporting it to your life.
  • How does this relate to your life?
  • Where do you see these passages in your life?
  • “In meditation we must welcome the word of God into our lives
    • and turn it from a seemingly dead word to it’s own truthful lively state.”
